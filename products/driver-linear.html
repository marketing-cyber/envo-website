KEY FEATURES

Slim Linear Form Factor

Aluminium extrusion housing fits flush in narrow cabinet bays — replaces legacy bulky drivers without retrofit.

Universal AC Input

100–277V auto-ranging — single SKU works in US, EU and AU markets without binning.

IP67 Sealed

Resin-potted internals — install outdoors, in canopies, or above-ceiling without weatherproofing.

Constant Voltage Output

Stable 12V / 24V / 48V regulated output — works with all ENVO modules and any standard CV LED.

Built-In Protections

Over-voltage, over-current, over-temperature and short-circuit protection on every channel.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · CB certifications and ENVO global support.

AVAILABLE VARIANTS
