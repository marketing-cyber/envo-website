KEY FEATURES

Diamondback 170° Optic

Wide-angle lens spreads light evenly across the sign face — fewer hot spots, fewer modules per square metre.

3 CCT Options

3000K warm, 4000K natural and 7000K cool — pick the temperature that matches your brand.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted PCB and silicone-sealed lens survive humidity, dust and direct rain.

Fast to Install

3M VHB adhesive backing, pre-stripped tinned leads and male/female plug connectors.

Up to 20 in Series

Long single-driver runs reduce splice points and keep the install tidy.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · CB · LM-80 · BIS · RoHS certifications and ENVO global support.

AVAILABLE VARIANTS

Four LED-count variants in the EcoGlo line. Pick by sign size and depth — more LEDs = brighter and deeper coverage. Each variant is available in 3000K / 4000K / 7000K.

EV-BLEG01LBY

Single LED

  • 0.4W · 1 × SMD2835
  • Shallow letters (40–60 mm)
  • Most economical option
EV-BLEG02LBY

Double LED

  • 0.8W · 2 × SMD2835
  • Standard letters (60–90 mm)
  • Most popular for indoor signs
EV-BLEG03LBY

Triple LED

  • 1.2W · 3 × SMD2835
  • Mid-depth letters (90–120 mm)
  • Bright daytime visibility
EV-BLEG04LBY

Quad LED

  • 1.6W · 4 × SMD2835
  • Deep letters / lightboxes (120–150 mm)
  • Maximum brightness in series

Add suffix -WW (3000K), -NW (4000K) or -CW (7000K) to the model number to specify CCT.
