EV-SP-TDM · TRIAC DIMMABLE

Triac Dimmable LED Driver

When the install needs dimming control, but the budget doesn't stretch to 0–10V or DALI gear, EV-SP-TDM is the answer. Six SKUs across 30W / 60W / 80W output and 12V / 24V — fully compatible with any standard mains-side triac dimmer (Lutron, Leviton, Clipsal, Crabtree). Wire it like a regular driver, dim it like a regular light.

KEY FEATURES

Triac Dimmer Compatible

Works with any standard mains-side triac dimmer — drop-in for retrofit jobs without rewiring control circuits.

5–100% Dimming Range

Smooth output from full bright down to mood-light low — no popping, no flicker, no minimum-load tricks.

DIY-Friendly Wiring

Standard mains in, low-voltage DC out — no separate dim wire, no programming, no commissioning.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted internals — install indoors, in cabinets or in protected outdoor locations.

Both Phase-Cut Modes

Forward (leading-edge) and reverse (trailing-edge) phase compatibility — works with old and new dimmer stock.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV certifications and ENVO global support.
