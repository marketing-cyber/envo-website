EV-SE & EV-SNPV · SCREW TERMINAL

Screw Terminal LED Driver

For installs where speed beats fancy — open the lid, strip the wire, twist the screw, you're done. Two compact non-dimmable families: EV-SE (outdoor-rated, 15W to 75W) and EV-SNPV (indoor cabinet-style, 40W and 60W). Both 12V or 24V. Both designed for the install where a fancy IP67 module driver is overkill.

KEY FEATURES

Tool-Free Wiring

Strip, insert, tighten the screw — wire is clamped and held. No soldering, no crimping, no special pliers.

Two Form Factors

EV-SE for outdoor sealed installs · EV-SNPV for indoor cabinet bays — pick by environment, not application.

Non-Dimmable, Always On

Set-and-forget power supplies — no dim wire, no commissioning, no compatibility matrix.

Built-In Protections

Over-voltage, over-current, over-temperature and short-circuit protection on every output.

Sealed Outdoor Variant

EV-SE is IP67 — install behind cabinets, in canopies or directly on the wall outdoors.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV certifications and ENVO global support.
