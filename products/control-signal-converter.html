KEY FEATURES

Two-in-One DALI Bridge

The EV-ZBDA-2421 converts ZigBee to both DALI and 0/1-10V from one device — common in retrofit halls.

Standard In-Wall Form

Switches and dimmers fit standard double / triple gang back-boxes — drop-in replacement for legacy switches.

AC Phase Dimming

Mains-side phase dimmer for incandescent / MLV / ELV loads — 1-channel and mini in-wall variants.

Blinds & Curtain Control

Dedicated SKU for motorised blinds — on/off and level positioning over ZigBee.

ZigBee 3.0 Mesh

Devices repeat the network — converters extend mesh coverage beyond the gateway's range.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV certifications and ENVO global support.
