Sidelit Single-LED Modules

Side-emitting LEDs throw light parallel to the sign face, not into it — that means you can build lightboxes as shallow as 25 mm and edge-lit panels that stay perfectly even across the entire surface. Three Sidelit families, scaling from compact retail use up to architectural-grade output.

KEY FEATURES

Edge-Lit Architecture

LEDs throw light sideways across the sign face — perfectly even illumination across panels too shallow for backlit modules.

Shallow Lightboxes

Build cabinets 25–80 mm deep — 30% slimmer than equivalent backlit installs.

3 CCT Options

3000K warm, 4000K natural and 7000K cool — pick the temperature that matches your brand.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted PCB and silicone-sealed lens survive humidity, dust and direct rain.

3 Power Tiers

Step up from EdgeLume to EdgeFlare to EdgeBlade as panel width and brightness demand grows.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · LM-80 · RoHS certifications and ENVO global support.
