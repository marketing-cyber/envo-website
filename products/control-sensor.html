KEY FEATURES

PIR & Microwave

Pick PIR (line-of-sight, false-trigger-rare) or microwave (through-glass, wide-angle) — both supported.

Multi-Sensor 4-in-1

Motion, ambient lux, temperature and humidity from one node — fewer SKUs to stock, fewer batteries to change.

IP65 Outdoor + ZHAGA

Outdoor microwave variant comes with the standardised ZHAGA Book 18 socket for streetlight retrofits.

0-10V Direct Dimming

Ceiling sensors output 0-10V directly — drive any 0-10V driver without an extra ZigBee gateway.

Door & Window State

Magnetic open/close detection — security alerts, after-hours signage triggers, HVAC control.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV certifications and ENVO global support.
