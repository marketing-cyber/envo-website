KEY FEATURES

Ultra-Compact Footprint

The smallest module in the line-up — slips into channel letters where Eco or Pro modules simply won't fit.

3 CCT Options

3000K warm, 4000K natural and 7000K cool — pick the temperature that matches your brand.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted PCB and silicone-sealed lens survive humidity, dust and direct rain.

170° Wide Beam

Diamondback optics keep illumination even across narrow letter faces — no scalloping or hot spots.

Up to 25 in Series

Lower per-module wattage means longer runs on a single driver — fewer splice points, cleaner installs.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · LM-80 · RoHS certifications and ENVO global support.

AVAILABLE VARIANTS

Three LED-count variants in the MiniLux line. Each available in 3000K / 4000K / 7000K.

MiniLux Single

Single LED

  • 0.24W · 1 × SMD2835
  • Letter strokes 15–25 mm
  • Border accents and trims
MiniLux Duo

Duo LED

  • 0.48W · 2 × SMD2835
  • Narrow channel letters (25–50 mm)
  • Most popular Mini variant
MiniLux Triple

Triple LED

  • 0.72W · 3 × SMD2835
  • Compact letters (50–80 mm)
  • Brightest in the Mini line

Add suffix -WW (3000K), -NW (4000K) or -CW (7000K) to the model number to specify CCT.
