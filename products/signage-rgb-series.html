ChromaFlux RGB & RGBW Modules

Full-spectrum colour for signs that need to do more than glow. ChromaFlux backlit modules combine red, green and blue LEDs (plus optional pure-white channel) in a single Triple-LED package — drive with any standard RGB or RGBW controller for static colour, fades, chases or video-sync animation.

KEY FEATURES

16M True Colour

R/G/B channels mix to any colour in the spectrum — from brand-exact reds to subtle pastels.

RGBW Pure-White Option

The RGBW variant adds a dedicated white channel — cleaner whites and CRI-accurate brand colours.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted PCB and silicone-sealed lens survive humidity, dust and direct rain.

Standard 12V Drive

Works with any common-anode 12V RGB or RGBW controller — no proprietary protocol lock-in.

DMX / ZigBee / RF Ready

Pair with ENVO Control Gear for video-sync, scene presets or app-based dimming.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · LM-80 · RoHS certifications and ENVO global support.
