OptiLume 24V Backlit Modules

Twice the voltage, half the current — at 24V the same wattage moves through thinner wire with less voltage drop, letting you wire long sign runs without splice points or mid-line drivers. OptiLume Double and Quad-LED variants deliver the same Diamondback optic as Eco, optimised for runs above 10 metres.

KEY FEATURES

Up to 40 in Series

24V architecture doubles the practical run length on a single driver — fewer splices, less BOM, faster install.

Lower Voltage Drop

Half the current means thinner wire delivers cleaner power across long runs — visibly even brightness end-to-end.

3 CCT Options

3000K warm, 4000K natural and 6500K cool — pick the temperature that matches your brand.

IP65 Sealed

Resin-potted PCB and silicone-sealed lens survive humidity, dust and direct rain.

170° Wide Beam

Diamondback optic — the same even-coverage profile used across the ENVO module range.

5-Year Warranty

Backed by UL · CE · TUV · LM-80 · RoHS certifications and ENVO global support.
