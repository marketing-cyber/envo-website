AVAILABLE VARIANTS

Two Pro families — UltraFlare (high-output SMD3030) and ProGlo (balanced SMD2835). Each available in Single / Double / Triple / Quad LED.

UltraFlare Quad

UltraFlare — Max Output

  • ~2W per module · 4 × SMD3030
  • Deep lightboxes, large channel letters
  • Daylight-readable signs
ProGlo Quad

ProGlo — Balanced

  • ~1.95W per module · 4 × SMD2835
  • Mid-depth lightboxes (90–150 mm)
  • Better lumens-per-watt
UltraFlare Triple

UltraFlare — Triple LED

  • ~1.63W per module · 3 × SMD3030
  • Standard channel letters (80–120 mm)
  • Most popular Pro variant
UltraFlare Single

UltraFlare — Single LED

  • ~0.86W per module · 1 × SMD3030
  • Narrow Pro applications
  • Pro brightness, low power

Both UltraFlare and ProGlo offered in Single / Double / Triple / Quad LED counts. Add CCT suffix to specify temperature.
